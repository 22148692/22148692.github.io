Celtec Games is an independent game studio passionate about creating immersive, creative, and memorable gaming experiences. We focus on original ideas, strong storytelling, and engaging gameplay that brings players together. From early concepts to polished releases, our goal is simple: make games that are fun, meaningful, and built with the community at heart.

Our community matters most and we take feedback and host regular Q&As so that fans have a say in what is done at this company and can help our games succeed among our variety of audiences!
